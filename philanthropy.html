Our Mission

As a Brother of Kappa Delta Rho, one of our core values is to, "Engage in the service of mankind, not for the praise or recognition that such service may bring, but because it is the right thing to do;" and, at the Lambda Beta Chapter here at James Madison University, we strive to acomplish this every semester by rasing money and awareness for the various causes we support.

The Organizations We Support

  • B+ Foundation:Helps support children with cancer through family assitance, funding research, and advocacy.
  • Wounded Warrior Project:Supports veterans who have been injured fighting for our country by giving them a support network to help them.
  • JMU Relay for Life:An annual school wide fundraisng event here at JMU, ran by the American cancer society, which raises money for those affected by cancer

How We Support Them

  • For the Fall semester we focus on rasing money for B+, our National Fraternity's philanthropy, to do this we do events on campus like: Pie A Brother Bake Sales Dog Petting on the Quad, as well as a Powder Puff Football tournament in which differnt soroities come and play flag fooball against eachother.
  • In the Spring we focus on the Wounded Warrior Project by having a Pig Roast at one of our houses and rasing money through that event. And with Relay for life also being in the spring we raise money for our KDR team through donations from others and by other various small events, ending with the whole Brotherhood showing up to praticipate elay for the whole night.
  • For every semester we set up a donation link where parents and alumni can donate to our causes Donate
