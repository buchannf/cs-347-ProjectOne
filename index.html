A Message from Our Consol:

You are going to see this poll floating around A LOT before school starts back up... 2020 is FUBAR, but that doesn't mean we should give up.

While other fraternities go "dormant" we can, and will, find creative ways to safely strengthen our brotherhood.

Innovation is the only way we can succeed.

I am preparing to unveil my goals for our chapter for 2020, but every bit of creativity that our brotherhood pitches in can make this job easier.

Jimmy Cater's presidential campaign motto was created by the janitor at his favorite diner.

We desperately need "Out of the box" ideas from each and every one of you.

Time for us to "improvise, adapt, and overcome"

- Kappa Delta Rho is about to make 2020 our bitch.
