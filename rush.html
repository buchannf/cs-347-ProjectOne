Why Kappa Delta Rho?

Kappa Delta Rho gives young men the opportunity to become the best man they can be by:

  • Giving opportunities to build leadership skills.
  • Become involed on campus and off campus, through philanthropy and community service.
  • Building connections with undergraduate brothers and alumni that can last a life time.
